Data Portal

Providing Access to Child Welfare Data to Encourage Better Decisions With today’s technology, we can do so much more to help people easily access and understand data – going beyond complex spreadsheets with rows and rows of numbers. Through a data-sharing agreement with the Washington DSHS Children’s Administration, Partners for Our Children developed the Washington State Child Well-Being Data Portal– a powerful web tool that provides access to data about children and families who interact with the child welfare system.
